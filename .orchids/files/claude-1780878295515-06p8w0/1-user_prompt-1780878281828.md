## Hero Video Requirements

Replace the traditional hero background image with a premium full-screen video background.

### Video Style

Use a high-quality looping background video featuring:

* Semi trucks driving on major interstate highways
* Drone footage tracking freight trucks
* Flatbed trucks transporting cargo
* Long-haul trucking operations
* Transportation and logistics scenes
* Highway travel at sunrise or sunset
* Professional freight movement across America

The video should communicate:

* Nationwide freight transportation
* Reliability
* Professionalism
* Safety
* Movement and connectivity

### Hero Layout

Full viewport height (100vh)

Video should:

* Autoplay
* Be muted
* Loop continuously
* Play inline on mobile
* Have a dark gradient overlay for readability

Overlay example:

Linear gradient:
rgba(0,0,0,0.55) → rgba(0,0,0,0.70)
